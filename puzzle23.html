Puzzle 23: Known-Plaintext Attack

An encrypted flag was intercepted. You know the flag starts with "FLAG{" and uses single-byte XOR encryption.

Encrypted Flag (hex):
0E 0C 03 01 68 2D 29 2C 2C 29

Known Plaintext: "FLAG{"

Known-Plaintext Attack:
  - Encrypted[0] = 0x0E
  - Plaintext[0] = 'F' = 0x46
  - Key = 0x0E XOR 0x46 = ?

Once you find the key, decrypt the entire message.

What is the decrypted flag?

XOR the first encrypted byte with 'F' to find the key. Then XOR all encrypted bytes with that key.
