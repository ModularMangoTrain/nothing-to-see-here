Puzzle 17: Memory Offsets

A memory dump shows data at various offsets. Read the ASCII string starting at offset 0x00000020.

00000000: FF FF FF FF 00 00 00 00 12 34 56 78 90 AB CD EF
00000010: 00 00 00 00 FF FF FF FF AA BB CC DD EE FF 00 11
00000020: 53 50 41 47 48 45 54 54 49 00 00 00 00 00 00 00
00000030: 00 00 00 00 00 00 00 00 FF FF FF FF 00 00 00 00

What word is at offset 0x00000020?

Read bytes at offset 0x20 and convert to ASCII.
